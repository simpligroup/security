# 🔐 GrupoSimpli Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

**Last updated:** August 6, 2025  
**Effective until:** October 31, 2026

At **GrupoSimpli**, we are committed to maintaining the security and privacy of our users, customers, and partners. We believe that responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities helps us ensure the safety of our platforms and services.

This policy explains how to report vulnerabilities and outlines the scope, rules of engagement, and legal safe harbor for security researchers.

---

## 📍 Domains in Scope

- `*.gruposimpli.com`
- `*.simplitec.io`
- `*.simplimuv.com`
- All systems and services owned, operated, or maintained by GrupoSimpli that are publicly accessible.

---

## 📬 How to Report a Vulnerability

Please report vulnerabilities by emailing:

**📧 [devsecops@gruposimpli.com](mailto:devsecops@gruposimpli.com)**

We **strongly encourage** the use of encryption. Our PGP public key is available here:

**🔐 [https://simpligroup.github.io/security/publickey.asc](https://simpligroup.github.io/security/publickey.asc)**

We accept reports in **English** or **Spanish**.

Please include:

- A clear and concise description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Affected system, URL, or component
- Your contact information (optional)

---

## 🔍 Scope

### ✅ In Scope
- Web apps, APIs, and services on the listed domains
- Mobile applications published under GrupoSimpli
- Authentication, authorization, and session management mechanisms

### ❌ Out of Scope
- DoS/DDoS attacks or brute force attempts
- Social engineering or physical access
- Vulnerabilities in third-party services not controlled by GrupoSimpli
- Issues without a clear security impact
- Clickjacking on non-sensitive endpoints
- Rate limiting or CAPTCHA bypasses without proven exploitability

---

## 🛡️ Rules of Engagement

- Only test assets you own or are authorized to test
- Avoid compromising data, disrupting services, or affecting other users
- Avoid accessing or modifying data that is not yours
- Immediately report any access to sensitive data
- Do **not** use automated destructive tools

---

## 📅 Our Commitment

- Acknowledge your report within **5 business days**
- Provide updates at least every 10 business days
- Remediate valid findings in a timely manner
- Offer public acknowledgment in our Hall of Fame (with your consent)

**No monetary rewards** at this time.

---

## 🏅 Hall of Fame

Valid contributors may be listed in our  
[Security Hall of Fame]((https://simpligroup.github.io/security/hall-of-fame.md).

---

## ⚖️ Safe Harbor

We will not take legal action against individuals who:

- Follow this policy in good faith
- Avoid privacy violations, service disruption, or data exfiltration
- Report vulnerabilities responsibly
